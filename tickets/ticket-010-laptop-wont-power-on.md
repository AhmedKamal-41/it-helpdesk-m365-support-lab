## User Description
"My laptop won't turn on at all. No lights, no sound, nothing happens when I press power."

## Business Impact
User has no working device. Fully blocked until repaired or replaced.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirmed the power adapter was plugged into a working outlet (tested outlet with another device).
2. Checked the charger light — no LED, and no charging light on the laptop.
3. Swapped in a known-good charger of the same model — laptop showed a charge light and powered on.
4. Original charger confirmed faulty (no output).
5. **Escalated to Level 2** for hardware replacement approval of the charger and to log the asset.

## Root Cause
Faulty AC power adapter — the laptop battery had fully drained and the dead charger could not power or charge it.

## Resolution
Replaced the faulty charger with a known-good unit; laptop booted normally. IT Manager approved a replacement charger from spare inventory and updated the asset record.

## User-Facing Final Response
> Hi Mia, good news — your laptop is fine; the charger had failed. I've given you a replacement charger and your laptop is back up and charging. Please recycle the old one with me so it doesn't get mixed up. — IT Help Desk

## Prevention / Follow-Up Notes
- **Escalation reason:** Hardware replacement/asset approval is a Level 2 task.
- Faulty charger tagged and removed from circulation.
